Cakes in SF: Dianda's Bakery

Located in the heart of the Mission District of San Francisco, Dianda's Bakery is a purveyor of classic Italian pastries. Dianda's brings 56 years of baking experience, passion for taste and a respect for tradition. Dianda's Bakery was opened by Mr. and Mrs. Dianda in 1962. They brought their favorite recipes from their hometown in Lucca, northern Italy. Their goal, bring a taste of Italy to San Francisco, their new home. The Dianda's raised three sons, who carried their legacy forward expanding the business to two Bay Area locations, supplying beloved baked goods to people across the Bay.
